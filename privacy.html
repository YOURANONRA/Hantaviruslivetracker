EverythingToday.in is a public surveillance dashboard. We do not require user accounts, names, or emails to access our Hanta Virus Tracker. We collect minimal non-personal data through standard server logs (IP addresses) to monitor site traffic.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Our tracker links to official health organizations (WHO, CDC). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.
