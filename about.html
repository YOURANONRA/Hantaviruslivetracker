About the Tracker

EverythingToday.in is an independent digital surveillance unit focused on real-time pathogen tracking and public health data visualization.

Our Hanta Virus Tracker was launched in May 2026 in response to the Andes Virus (ANDV) cluster identified on the MV Hondius cruise ship. We believe that during a public health crisis, information should be fast, free, and accessible without the friction of paywalls or complex government portals.

Real-Time Data

We aggregate reports from IHR Focal Points and the WHO Disease Outbreak News (DONs).

Global Focus

While based in India, our tracking spans 12+ countries currently monitoring ANDV transmission.

We remain committed to the highest standards of data integrity and transparency.
